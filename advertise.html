Advertising Opportunities

The chorus offers advertisement opportunities both in their concert programs and fundraising efforts. We have two advertising packages to offer: Seasonal and Single.

Seasonal Ads

  • Ad in our Holiday Concert, reaching over 500 attendees
  • Ad in our Pride Concert , reaching over 800 attendees
  • Your business listed at our Benefit Musicale, reaching over 200 influential individuals in the area
  • Logo and Contact information listed on our website during the entire season

Single Ads

  • Ad in our Holiday Concert or Pride Concert
  • Logo and Contact information listed on our website 30 days before and after the chosen concert
