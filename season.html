I am Harvey Milk

Saturday, June 7, 2014

LIVE THE LEGACY. The DGMC invites you to attend our premiere of the co-commissioned oratorio, "I am Harvey Milk". Part choral work, part theatre piece, with music and lyrics by Tony Award-nominated composer Andrew Lippa. "I am Harvey Milk" is an emotional celebration of an unforgettable American Icon.
